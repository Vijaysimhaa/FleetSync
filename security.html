FleetSync Password Policy

To ensure the security of your account, passwords must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum length of 8 characters.
  • Must contain at least one uppercase letter.
  • Must contain at least one lowercase letter.
  • Must contain at least one number.
  • Symbols are optional but recommended for stronger security.

It is recommended to use a unique password for your FleetSync account that is not used on other websites or applications.
